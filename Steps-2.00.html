Getting started with Qlik sense 2
  • To get started we have to log in with our user name and password.? 



  • When Qlik Sense Desktop starts up, you arrive at the hub. The hub is where you find all your apps. Click an app in the hub to open it in a separate tab or create a new app.
The Qlik Sense Desktop hub with the Work view open.


  • Name it Auto Repair Shop and click create.



  • Select Open app to add data.


You start Qlik Sense by entering a web address in your browser, such as https://<server name>/hub. If you are not using Windows, a login window will open in your browser. Enter your User name in the format DOMAIN\user.

The exact address and credentials that you enter depend on how your organization has deployed Qlik Sense.

Qlik Sense opens at the hub, which is where you see all the apps you have access to. If there are no apps, you can begin by creating your first app.
